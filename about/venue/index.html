Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre

The Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre is centrally located near the Melbourne CBD with easy access to public transport. It's a short walk from both Southern Cross station (1.2km) and Flinders St station (1.6km), and serviced by the 12, 96 and 109 trams.

Address: 1 Convention Centre Pl, South Wharf, Melbourne Victoria, Australia 3006
